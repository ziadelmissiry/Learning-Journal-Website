Tutorial 1: How to undertake online training successfully?

  1. Step 1: Work on yourself before you start working

    • Wake up early
    • Have a morning routine
    • Eat breakfast

  2. Step 2: It’s time to start working

    • Minimize distractions
    • Close irrelevant tabs on your browser
    • Have all your supplies ready next to you

  3. Step 3: Work

    Attend online meetings and stay in contact with your colleagues, maybe suggest creating a group chat. Finish your work as soon as you can you're still a trainee and you want to stand out.

  4. Step 4: Ask for help

    Don’t be afraid to ask for help or feedback from your boss or colleagues; they’ll be more than happy to help you.

  5. Step 5: Educate Yourself

    You're still a trainee here to learn, always remember that. Sometimes you'll need to take your education into your own hands. Use online resources like Udemy or Khan Academy.

  6. Step 6: Optimize

    After understanding your tasks, try to optimize them. Look for apps to do your work so you work smart not hard, this will save you so much time. I suggest using a program called UiPath to create bots that might help you automate daily repetitive work.

Tutorial 2: How to keep in touch with colleagues?

When it’s appropriate, ask your boss if you can make an announcement to all your colleagues in an online meeting. Ask your colleagues how they'd like to be contacted, WhatsApp is always a good suggestion. It’s better to have an informal group chat so you and your colleagues can bond and help each other. Organize socials between you and your colleagues on the group chat, maybe even create a discord so you can all game together. However, you have to respect your colleagues and avoid vulgar or inappropriate comments on the group chat or you might get reported to HR.

Tutorial 3:Considering a carrer in web development

Some people believe that web development is a gift. However, it’s not a skill you can learn just like driving. In other words, you don’t have to be an artist or gifted to make a website look nice, there are certain rules you have to follow. Now that that’s out of the way, how do you get started? First, you have to decide whether you want to be a back-end or a front end developer or both.

Front-end developers are the ones that design everything you see on the website and focus on the user interface, also known as UI. Front- end developers usually use these languages:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

Back end developers focus on functionality. They do all the behind the scene work like creating libraries and API’s. Back-end developes usually use these coding languages:

  • PHP
  • Python
  • Java
